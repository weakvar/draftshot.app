Meet Draftshot

ML-based app that predicts how much popular might be your upcoming shots on Dribbble

Shot by Halo Graphic

Draftshot uses machine learning to predict popularity of any design. Whether it’s a logo, web design or illustration, it doesn’t matter. Draftshot will help you to create a post on Dribbble that will be super popular among designers.
